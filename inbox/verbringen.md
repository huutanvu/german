# verbringen

## Meanings
- to spend (time)
- to pass (time)

## Grammar
- Part of speech: Verb (strong, irregular, inseparable)
- Perfekt: hat verbracht
- Conjugation:
  - ich verbringe
  - du verbringst
  - er/sie/es verbringt
  - wir verbringen
  - ihr verbringt
  - sie/Sie verbringen
  - Past tense (Präteritum): verbrachte

## Daily Use Case
- Ich verbringe meinen Urlaub am Meer.

## Professional Use Case
- Wir verbringen viel Zeit in Meetings.

## Tips
- Always used with time-related accusative objects (e.g., Zeit, Urlaub, Stunden).
- The prefix "ver-" is inseparable, so the past participle is "verbracht".

## Caution
- Do not confuse with "ausgeben" (to spend money). Use "verbringen" only for time, and "ausgeben" for money.
